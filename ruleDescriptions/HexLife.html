Hexagonal Life

Same as the Game of Life rule, but on a hexagonal lattice. See the Life rule description for more details.

Cells are either occupied (live) or unoccupied (dead) and evolve according to the following rules.

  • A live cell with fewer than 2 live neighbors dies of loneliness.
  • A live cell with 4 or more live neighbors dies of overcrowding.
  • A live cell with exactly 2 or 3 live neighbors survives unchanged.
  • A dead cell with exactly 3 live neighbors will come to life (birth).
